Zlatan Ibrahimovic

Zlatan Ibrahimović is a Swedish professional footballer who plays as a striker for Serie A club AC Milan and the Sweden national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest strikers of all time. Ibrahimović is one of the most decorated active footballers in the world, having won 31 trophies in his career.
Born: 3 October 1981 (age 40 years), Malmö
Height: 1.95 m
Partner: Helena Seger
Salary: 70 lakhs EUR (2021)
Current teams: A.C. Milan (#11 / Forward), Sweden national football team (Forward)
Children: Maximilian Ibrahimović, Vincent Ibrahimović
Nationality: Bosnian, Swedish
